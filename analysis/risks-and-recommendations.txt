1. DNS - 53 port open on host.
Risk: Can be abused for amplification attacks or info leaks.

2. http - 80 port open on host.
Risk: Risk of sniffing, outdated servers, XSS etc.

3. https - 443 port open on host.
Risk: Check for weak ciphers, expired certs

4. http-alt - 8000 port open on host.
Risk: Can be misconfigured, may lack auth

5. https-alt - 8443 port open on host.
Risk: Frequently exposed admin portals

6. sdr - 9010 port open on host.
Risk: If no authentication is in place, attackers may view or control SDR services
